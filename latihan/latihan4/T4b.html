Arknights Brings EP 14: "Absolved Will Be the Seekers" to Ignite Your Passion!

November 1st, 2024 - Shanghai, China - Arknights Global is setting the stage ablaze with its spectacular EP 14 "Absolved Will Be the Seekers"! Get ready for an unforgettable experience featuring the debut of new Operators, exciting challenges, and a variety of other activities. Be sure to mark your calendars from October 31st, 10:00 (UTC-7) to November 14th, 03:59 (UTC-7), and join us in experiencing this remarkable milestone for Arknights Global. Dive into the excitement—this is one event you won't want to miss!

Doctors can explore various stages such as "Absolved Will Be the Seekers," "Hidden Front," and "Secret Place" to complete commissions and earn "Emergency Inhibitors." These inhibitors can be exchanged at the "Strategic Arsenal" for a vast array of valuable items, including the 5-star Operator Scene, the Airship Abode furniture set, Headhunting Permits, Elite Materials, LMD, and more. These rewards will significantly enhance your in-game experience by making upgrading and collecting easier and more enjoyable.

Additionally, by completing the Episode 14 "Absolved Will Be the Seekers" stages and “Hidden Front” missions, Doctors can unlock confidential recordings in ”Secret Place.” Achieving this will allow Doctors to obtain the 6-star Operator Civilight Eterna for free. Don’t miss out on these fantastic opportunities to elevate your gameplay!

NEW OPERATORS


6-star Flinger Operator Wiš'adel:
Wiš'adel brings unique talents to the battlefield. Her attacks have increased ATK against the main target and attach an afterimage to it. These afterimages can explode when affected by Wiš'adel's aftershock, dealing Physical damage to all nearby enemies and stunning them for a short time. Upon deployment, Wiš'adel immediately summons a Revenant's Shadow within her attack range and gains Camouflage when near it.

6-star Core Caster Operator Logos:
Logos deals Arts damage, and his unique talent allows him to further amplify his impact: when attacking a target, he has a chance to also strike another random target within his Attack Range, dealing Arts damage and Slowing them for a certain duration.

6-star Bard Civilight Eterna:
Civilight Eterna as a support unit, she does not attack but continuously restores the HP of all allies within range, equal to 10% of her ATK per second. She is unaffected by Inspiration.

5-star Charger Fang the Fire-sharpened:
Fang the Fire-sharpened obtains 1 DP after defeating an enemy and refunds the original DP cost when retreated. Her talent reduces the DP cost of her first deployment, and upon retreat, it also reduces the DP cost of the next deployed operator.
